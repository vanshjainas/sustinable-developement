Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

Why It Matters

Our planet is running out of resources, and our current consumption and production patterns are unsustainable. Responsible consumption and production are crucial for sustainable development.

How You Can Help

Here are a few ways you can contribute to responsible consumption and production:

  • Reduce waste by recycling and reusing products.
  • Support companies and products that follow sustainable practices.
  • Educate others about the importance of sustainable consumption and production.

More info

Sustainable Development Goal 12 (SDG 12) aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns. This goal is crucial for reducing our ecological footprint and ensuring that future generations can meet their needs.

SDG 12 targets include achieving the sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources, reducing waste generation through prevention, reduction, recycling, and reuse, and encouraging companies to adopt sustainable practices and integrate sustainability information into their reporting cycle.

Significant progress has been made in promoting responsible consumption and production. However, challenges such as excessive waste generation, resource depletion, and unsustainable consumption patterns persist, particularly in developed countries.

Addressing these challenges requires a comprehensive approach that includes promoting sustainable resource management, supporting sustainable business practices, encouraging consumer awareness and education, and implementing policies that support sustainable consumption and production. Collaboration among governments, international organizations, civil society, and the private sector is essential for achieving SDG 12.
