Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.

Why It Matters

Access to energy is crucial for the advancement of society. It impacts all aspects of development, from job creation to economic growth, security concerns, and environmental protection.

How You Can Help

Here are a few ways you can contribute to promoting affordable and clean energy:

  • Support initiatives and organizations working to improve access to clean and affordable energy.
  • Volunteer your time and skills to renewable energy projects and conservation campaigns.
  • Advocate for policies that promote sustainable energy solutions and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

More info

Sustainable Development Goal 7 (SDG 7) aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. Energy is central to nearly every major challenge and opportunity the world faces today, from the availability of jobs to security concerns, climate change, food production, and increasing incomes.

SDG 7 targets include ensuring universal access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy services, increasing the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix, and improving energy efficiency.

Significant progress has been made in increasing access to electricity and improving energy efficiency. However, challenges such as reliance on fossil fuels, inadequate infrastructure, and disparities in energy access persist. These issues are particularly pronounced in developing countries and rural areas.

Addressing these challenges requires a comprehensive approach that includes investing in renewable energy infrastructure, promoting energy efficiency practices, supporting research and development in clean energy technologies, and ensuring equitable access to energy services. Collaboration among governments, international organizations, civil society, and the private sector is essential for achieving SDG 7.
