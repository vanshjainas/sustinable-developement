Goal 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all and build effective, accountable and inclusive institutions at all levels.

Why It Matters

Peace, justice, and strong institutions are essential for sustainable development. They are crucial for ensuring that all people enjoy rights and freedoms, and for promoting inclusive and peaceful societies.

How You Can Help

Here are a few ways you can contribute to promoting peace, justice, and strong institutions:

  • Support organizations that promote human rights and justice.
  • Advocate for transparency and accountability in government.
  • Participate in local governance and community decision-making processes.

More info

Sustainable Development Goal 16 (SDG 16) aims to promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions at all levels. Peace, justice, and strong institutions are essential for sustainable development. They are crucial for ensuring that all people enjoy rights and freedoms and for promoting inclusive and peaceful societies.

SDG 16 targets include significantly reducing all forms of violence, ending abuse, exploitation, trafficking, and all forms of violence against and torture of children, promoting the rule of law at the national and international levels and ensuring equal access to justice for all, and substantially reducing corruption and bribery in all their forms.

Significant progress has been made in promoting peace, justice, and strong institutions, with many countries implementing reforms to improve governance, transparency, and accountability. However, challenges such as conflict, violence, and corruption persist, particularly in regions with weak institutions and limited resources.

Addressing these challenges requires a comprehensive approach that includes promoting human rights, strengthening institutions, ensuring access to justice, and fostering a culture of peace and non-violence. Collaboration among governments, international organizations, civil society, and the private sector is essential for achieving SDG 16.
