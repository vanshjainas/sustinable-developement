Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

Why It Matters

Economic growth should be a positive force for the whole planet. This means promoting policies that encourage entrepreneurship and job creation, while eradicating forced labor, slavery, and human trafficking.

How You Can Help

Here are a few ways you can contribute to promoting decent work and economic growth:

  • Support initiatives and organizations working to improve employment opportunities and economic conditions.
  • Volunteer your time and skills to projects aimed at economic development and job creation.
  • Advocate for policies that promote fair wages, safe working conditions, and inclusive economic growth.

More info

Sustainable Development Goal 8 (SDG 8) aims to promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. This goal is vital for fostering economic prosperity, reducing inequality, and ensuring fair and safe labor conditions globally.

SDG 8 targets include achieving higher levels of economic productivity through diversification, technological upgrading, and innovation, as well as promoting policies that support productive activities, decent job creation, entrepreneurship, creativity, and innovation.

Significant progress has been made in reducing unemployment rates and improving labor conditions. However, challenges such as underemployment, poor working conditions, and economic inequality persist, particularly in developing countries and among vulnerable populations.

Addressing these challenges requires a comprehensive approach that includes investing in education and skills development, promoting fair labor practices, supporting entrepreneurship and small businesses, and ensuring inclusive economic policies. Collaboration among governments, international organizations, civil society, and the private sector is essential for achieving SDG 8.
