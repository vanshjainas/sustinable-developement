Goal 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.

Why It Matters

Investments in infrastructure are crucial for achieving sustainable development. Innovation and industrialization are key drivers of economic growth and job creation.

How You Can Help

Here are a few ways you can contribute to promoting industry, innovation, and infrastructure:

  • Support initiatives and organizations working to improve infrastructure and foster innovation.
  • Volunteer your time and skills to projects aimed at industrial development and technological advancement.
  • Advocate for policies that promote sustainable industrialization and investment in infrastructure.

More info

Sustainable Development Goal 9 (SDG 9) aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation. These elements are essential for driving economic growth, creating jobs, and improving living standards.

SDG 9 targets include developing quality, reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure to support economic development and human well-being, promoting inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and enhancing scientific research and innovation.

Significant progress has been made in improving infrastructure and fostering innovation. However, challenges such as inadequate infrastructure, lack of investment in research and development, and disparities in industrial development persist, particularly in developing countries and rural areas.

Addressing these challenges requires a comprehensive approach that includes investing in infrastructure, supporting industrial development, promoting research and innovation, and ensuring inclusive economic policies. Collaboration among governments, international organizations, civil society, and the private sector is essential for achieving SDG 9.
